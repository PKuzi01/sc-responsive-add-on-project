Who is this guy?

Tyler, the Creator (born Tyler Gregory Okonma on March 6, 1991) is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and music video director. He first gained prominence as the leader of the alternative hip-hop collective Odd Future Wolf Gang Kill Them All (OFWGKTA), and has since released several successful solo albums.Tyler's music often incorporates themes of rebellion, personal struggle, and a sense of humor that can be both dark and absurdist. He is known for his distinctive style, which blends elements of hip-hop, jazz, and funk, and his innovative production techniques.

In addition to his music career, Tyler is also a successful fashion designer, having launched his own clothing brand called Golf Wang in 2011. He has also directed music videos for artists such as ASAP Rocky, Vince Staples, and himself, and has made cameo appearances in several films and TV shows. Overall, Tyler, the Creator is widely regarded as one of the most influential and creative figures in modern hip-hop, and has been recognized for his work with numerous awards and nominations.
